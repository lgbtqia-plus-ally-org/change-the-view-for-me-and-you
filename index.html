ABOUT US

LGBTQIA+ ALLY ORG. is a non-profit organization that advocates for LGBTQIA+ rights and equality.

"Change the View, For Me and You" is a project from this organization that helps empower queer voices and perspectives through creative and enriching storytelling.

OUR MISSION

With our project, "Change the View, For Me and You", we aim to transform real-life queer experiences into informative and engaging content to raise awareness, encourage inclusivity, and to fight against the ongoing discrimination that is faced by people who are part of the community.

Despite Filipinos becoming more accepting to queer people as a whole, others still refuse to accept the fact that there's nothing wrong with being queer. Additionaly, people from the LGBTQIA+ community still face different forms of discrimination and harrassment in different sectors. Our organization helps to create a safe space for people who identify themselves as queer.
